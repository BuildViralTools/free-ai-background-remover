FAQ

Is this background remover free?

Yes. It is built as a free browser tool with no required signup and no watermark added to the transparent PNG export.

What happens to temporary cache?

Generated preview URLs are revoked after downloads or when you clear the workspace. The button also attempts to clear browser Cache Storage created by this app when the browser allows it.

Why is the first run slower?

The high quality AI background removal library and large model have to load first. After that, processing depends mostly on your device and image size.
